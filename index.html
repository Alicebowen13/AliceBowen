About Me

The Expert Behind the Strategy

Every day, thousands of books are published. Most of them disappear within weeks because their authors were told that writing a great book was enough. It is not. A great book without a great marketing strategy is like a billboard in the desert — no one sees it.

That is where I come in. I am Alice Bowen, and I have dedicated my career to one mission: making sure that exceptional books find exceptional audiences. I am not a generalist marketer who dabbles in book promotion on the side. Book marketing is my entire world. I understand the publishing ecosystem from the inside out — from Amazon's ever-changing algorithm to the psychology behind why a reader clicks "Buy Now" versus scrolling past your cover.

My philosophy is simple. I believe that every book, regardless of genre or the size of the author's platform, has a built-in audience waiting to discover it. My job is to find that audience, put your book directly in front of them, and give them every reason to buy it.

What makes me different from other marketers is my process. I do not believe in wasting time on lengthy phone calls that lead nowhere. Instead, I pour every ounce of my energy into the work that actually moves the needle — deep market research, precision ad targeting, metadata optimization, and strategic positioning. Everything I do is communicated via email, which means every strategy, every insight, and every result is documented in writing for you to review, reference, and build upon.

When you work with me, you are not hiring someone who will give you vague promises. You are partnering with a strategist who will give you a clear, actionable plan and then execute it with the kind of precision that turns unknown titles into category bestsellers.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

I believe in full transparency. Below are the questions I am asked most often by authors considering working with me.

Why do you only communicate via email?

I made the decision to operate exclusively via email for two important reasons. First, it allows me to dedicate the maximum amount of my working time to actually executing marketing strategies for my clients instead of spending hours each day on phone calls and video meetings. Second, email creates a permanent written record of every strategy, every recommendation, and every result. This means you can go back and reference any conversation at any time, which is something a phone call simply cannot provide. My clients consistently tell me that the email-only approach is not only efficient but actually preferable because it forces clarity and eliminates miscommunication.

How quickly will I see results from your marketing?

The timeline for results depends on several factors, including your book's genre, its current market position, the state of your existing metadata and advertising, and the specific strategy we implement. That said, most of my clients begin seeing measurable improvements in sales, rankings, or ad performance within the first 30 to 60 days. Some strategies, particularly those involving Amazon Ads, can show initial traction within the first two weeks. I always set realistic expectations upfront so you know exactly what to anticipate and when.

What genres do you specialize in?

I work with authors across a wide range of genres including romance, thriller, mystery, science fiction, fantasy, literary fiction, memoir, self-help, business, health and wellness, and more. Each genre has its own unique audience behavior, competitive dynamics, and marketing best practices, and I tailor my approach accordingly. Whether you write cozy mysteries or hard science fiction, I have the experience and the research methodology to find your ideal readers and put your book in front of them.

How do I get started working with you?

Getting started is simple. Scroll down to the contact form on this page and fill it out with your book details, or send me a direct email at alicebowen.marketer@gmail.com. Include your book title, genre, publication date, a brief description of your current marketing efforts, and what you are hoping to achieve. I will review your information and respond within 24 hours with my initial thoughts and next steps. There is no commitment required to send an inquiry — I am happy to discuss your situation and help you determine whether my services are the right fit.

What if I am a first-time author with no marketing experience?

First-time authors are not only welcome — they are some of my favorite clients to work with. The reason is simple: when you start with the right marketing foundation from the very beginning, you avoid the costly mistakes that many authors spend years trying to undo. I will guide you through every aspect of book marketing, explain everything in plain language, and build a strategy that is appropriate for where you are right now in your publishing journey. You do not need to come to me with marketing experience. You just need to come to me with a great book and the willingness to invest in its success.

What does your pricing look like?

My pricing varies depending on the scope of the project and the specific services you need. I do not publish fixed prices on my website because every author's situation is different, and I believe in providing a custom quote that reflects the actual work involved. Once you send me your inquiry and I have a clear picture of your goals and your book's current position, I will include transparent pricing in my proposal. I work with authors at various budget levels and I am always upfront about what is realistic to achieve within a given budget.

Can you help me if my book is already published and not selling well?

Absolutely. In fact, a significant portion of my work involves helping authors revitalize books that have already been published but are underperforming. Many books fail to sell not because they are bad books, but because their marketing was insufficient or poorly executed. Common issues I find include weak book descriptions, incorrect category placement, missing or ineffective keywords, and poorly structured ad campaigns. These are all fixable problems, and I have a proven track record of turning underperforming titles into consistent sellers.

Do you offer refunds if I am not satisfied with the results?

I stand behind the quality of my work and I am committed to delivering real, measurable results for every client. Before we begin any engagement, I set clear expectations about what you can realistically expect based on your book's genre, competition, and budget. That said, I understand that trust is earned, and I am always willing to discuss any concerns you have throughout our working relationship. My goal is your complete satisfaction, and I will work with you to make adjustments to the strategy if needed to ensure we are moving toward your goals.
